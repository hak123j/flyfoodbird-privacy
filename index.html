FlyFoodBird ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile game developed by Mrstge. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

📦 Information We Collect

Our app collects the following data through third-party advertising services:

  • Device Identifiers: We collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) for the purpose of serving personalized advertisements through Google AdMob.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

🔧 How We Use Your Information

The device identifier data we collect is used solely for:

🌐 Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google AdMob may collect and process data according to their own privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Device identifiers may be shared with our advertising partner (Google AdMob) for the purpose of delivering relevant advertisements.

🗄 Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Any data collected by third-party advertising services is subject to their respective data retention policies.

👶 Children's Privacy

FlyFoodBird is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

⚖ Your Rights

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
