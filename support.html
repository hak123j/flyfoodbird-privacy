❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Tap or click to make the bird fly up. Collect food to earn points and avoid obstacles. Explore different biomes and unlock new skins!

How do I unlock new skins?

Earn coins by collecting food during gameplay. Visit the in-game shop to browse and purchase skins with your earned coins.

Can I play offline?

Yes! FlyFoodBird works fully offline. An internet connection is only needed to display ads.

How do I remove ads?

Currently ads help keep the game free. You can watch rewarded ads voluntarily to earn bonus coins.

The game is crashing, what should I do?

Please make sure your app is updated to the latest version. If the problem persists, email us with your device model and OS version.
